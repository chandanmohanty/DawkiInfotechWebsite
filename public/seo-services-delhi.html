Honest answers

Questions before we talk?

How long until I see results?
Foundational technical wins surface in 30–45 days. Meaningful ranking and traffic movement on commercial keywords typically lands in months 3–6, depending on your starting authority and competition. We agree leading indicators for the first 90 days so you're never guessing.
Do you guarantee #1 rankings?
Anyone who guarantees position-1 on a competitive keyword is either lying or planning to use tactics that'll cost you a manual action. We guarantee process, transparency, and senior attention — and back it with quarterly contracts you can walk away from.
Will I keep the work if I leave?
Yes. Content, briefs, links, dashboards, scripts — all owned by you, in your accounts, from day one. No ransom dashboards, no proprietary lock-in.
Do you work with my existing tech stack?
We've shipped SEO on Next.js, Laravel, WordPress, Shopify, Magento, Webflow, Drupal, custom React SPAs, and headless setups with Sanity / Contentful. If your CMS is exotic, we'll usually find a way — or tell you honestly that we can't.
Do you handle content writing or just strategy?
Both. We have an in-house editorial team in Delhi for B2B, finance, healthcare, edtech, real estate, and D2C. For highly technical or regulated niches we use vetted subject-matter writers and our editors QA every piece.
What's the smallest engagement you take on?
Our Launch plan starts at ₹45k/month with a 90-day commitment. We don't do one-off audits below ₹65k — and we'll usually tell you when an audit isn't what you actually need.
